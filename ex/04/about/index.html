AD Creative is a young vibrant design company operating in the UK and China.


Founded by UK designer Kieron–Scott Woodhouse and China based Jerry Lao, AD Creative is at the forefront of developing innovative designed products by “Reinventing Simplicity”


The AD team meticulously designs every aspect of their products, including both software and hardware, to bring to market the perfect fusion of design and technology.


AD launched the ADzero Bamboo smartphone design concept at TENT London in 2011. The Team have been working solidly over the last year to enhance the design of the ADzero Bamboo smartphone and develop their own customised ADOAS operating system.

Kieron-Scott

Chief Designer / Co-Founder


Kieron-Scott is a Product Designer with a key interest and knowledge in mass-consumer based design. Having worked with a range of companies he has proven versatile and adaptive to any project thrown at him. Over the last two years his design input has been recognised and awarded by companies such as JosephJoseph and Estee Lauder as well as having some of his concept designs recognised throughout the online design community, In particular his ‘On Counter Technology’ re-casing which is currently used on Clinique counters throughout the UK including Harrods & Debenhams department stores. Kieron has just completed his final year at Middlesex University London in gaining a FIRST class Honours BA degree in Product Design which he has used to specifically focus on consumer related product development, branding and service design.
